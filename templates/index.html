Eat Smartly

Food production emissions make up more than a third of global greenhouse gas emissions. By adjusting our diets just a little bit, we can make a huge impact on the environment.

Eat healthy

The United States has a childhood obesity rate of about 20%. One reason for this is a lack of knowledge on what foods are healthy or not. Our application brings access to healthier recipes while also providing insights about others.

Recipe Analysis

Although the internet contains access to a wide variety of recipes, it is hard to know if they are truly healthy and environmental friendly. Our models can provide insights about these recipes by taking in ingredients and/or images and providing reports on how healthy and eco-friendly these recipes are.

How It Works
